About the Developer

Hi! I'm Emily Wallace, the creator of Sentimo. I'm passionate about building tools that help people understand and improve their emotional well-being. Sentimo was born from a desire to create a simple, private, and beautiful way to track mood patterns over time.


I believe that mental health tools should be accessible, private, and empowering. That's why Sentimo keeps all your data on your device and focuses on helping you discover your own patterns rather than telling you what to feel.


I'd love to hear from you! Whether you have feedback, questions, or just want to share your experience with Sentimo, please don't hesitate to reach out.
