Data Collection and Storage

Sentimo is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your mood data, voice recordings, and personal information are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

What Data We Store Locally

  • Mood ratings and timestamps
  • Optional voice recordings (stored as audio files)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Usage statistics for insights

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and passcode protection. We recommend keeping your device secure and updated.

Data Sharing

We never share, sell, or transmit your personal data to third parties. Your mood tracking information remains completely private to you.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the 'Reset All Data' option in Settings. You can also delete the app to remove all associated data from your device.
